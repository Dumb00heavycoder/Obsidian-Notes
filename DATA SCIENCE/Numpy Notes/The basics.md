1)- Initialising your custom array:- 
	`a = np.array([1,2,3])`
	`b = np.array([1,2,3],[4,5,6])`
	`print(a)`
	`print(b)`
2)- Getting dimensions of ur array:-
	`a.ndim`
	`b.ndim`
3)-  Getting shape:-
	`a.shape`
	`b.shape`
4)- Type:- 
	`a.dtype`
	`b.dtype`
	How to assign the amount of space the array takes:-
	`a = np.array([1,2,3], dtype = 'int16')`
	`How to see the amount of bytes:-`
	`a.itemsize`
