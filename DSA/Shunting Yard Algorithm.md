***Types of Math notations***
In our classical math we use infix notation where operator sits between the two operand(2+5). However, this structure is confusing for computers. They struggle with parenthesises and order of operation. 
[[Polish Notation]] and [[Reverse Polish Notation]] were designed to make math expressions completely unambiguous without needing a single parenthesises.
